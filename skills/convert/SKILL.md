# Skill: Convert

You are analyzing conversion signals and improving what turns visitors into players.

## Objective
Understand why people sign up or don't, and fix the biggest drop-off point in the funnel.

## Conversion Tasks

### Funnel Analysis
Map the current funnel:
- Where do people first hear about Copa? (which channel, which post)
- What do they see when they arrive? (landing page, game, social post)
- Where do they drop off?
- What does a converted user do that a bounced user doesn't?

### Signal Review
Check EXPERIMENTS.md and SIGNUPS.md for:
- Which posts or content generated the most signups?
- Which outreach messages got responses?
- What questions do new players ask that suggest confusion?
- Any retention signal — do players come back the next match day?

### Iteration
Based on analysis, propose ONE change to:
- Landing page copy or CTA
- Onboarding flow (first thing a new player does)
- Sharing mechanic (what makes them invite a friend)
- Scoring / game balance (if players report it's too hard or too easy)

## Output
Update SIGNUPS.md and METRICS.md with current numbers.
Update EXPERIMENTS.md with what was learned.
Propose any changes in ACTIONS.md as PENDING (if external action needed).
Document analysis in JOURNAL.md.
