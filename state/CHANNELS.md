## Channel Content & Execution Plan

### 1. WhatsApp / Telegram Groups (**Top Priority**)
**Pre-launch:** N/A  
**Launch week:** Internal seeding only (no public search). DMs to friend groups & football groups with sample Copa Cards (“Play with your group?”).  
**Tournament:** Players share post-match Cards directly in group chats. Group Organizers can buy Group Mode; drop Group Cards.  

**Content:**  
- Player-generated: Copa Cards (scorecards), Group Cards
- Copa-prompted: share CTA after each reveal (“Post in your group!”)
**Who posts:** Players themselves (viral loop).  

### 2. Twitter / X
**Pre-launch:** Copa account posts teaser card mockups, “not a prediction game” explainer thread, open waitlist.  
**Launch week:** Copa handles live-tweet Cards, match result reveals, memes, and creator retweets.  
**Tournament:** Daily Cards posted after every match (both from Copa and users), meme-quote responses to big calls. Pitch creators to share their Cards.

**Content:**  
- Copa Cards (visual), match debates, announcement threads  
**Who posts:** Copa + creators + users  
**Frequency:** 1–3 tweets per match; RT user Cards

### 3. Reddit (r/soccer, r/WorldCup, national subs)
**Pre-launch:** Meta threads with concept pitch—“Would you play this game?”  
**Launch week:** Match-day posts sharing how fans voted on key moments, aggregate Card analysis, meme response (“Copa crowd voted X on that VAR — did you?”)  
**Tournament:** Post-match topics for major games (“r/soccer, what did Copa Reads say about [incident]?”); AMA possible if mods approve.

**Content:**  
- Match Recap aggregated Cards, polls for crowd reactions, dedicated “how the fans saw it” reveals  
**Who posts:** Copa + possibly mods/users  
**Frequency:** Per big game/day; always fresh thread

### 4. Email Waitlist (owned)
**Pre-launch:** Collect signups—hero focus  
**Launch week:** Email announcement (“Go play Match 1 now!”)  
**Tournament:** Daily Cards sent per user for each match they play, with share CTA

**Content:**  
- “Your Card is Ready” mail + 1–2 other nudges for important matches  
**Who posts:** Copa (automated)  
**Frequency:** 1 per match played per user

### 5. Discord (football servers)
**Pre-launch:** DM 5+ server admins with group play/UGC pitch  
**Launch week:** One pinned Copa Card per partner server; encourage league/role sign-up  
**Tournament:** Copa Cards posted to results/World Cup channels

**Content:**  
- Group Card recaps, meme/contest triggers  
**Who posts:** Users + Copa (as guest)  
**Frequency:** Per match, when server is receptive

### 6. TikTok / IG Reels
**Pre-launch:** Submit Card templates to 3–5 micro-creators, demo “how to share Card reaction”  
**Launch week/tournament:** Seed first “Copa vs. My Friends” reaction with audio prompt  
**Content:**  
- Card reaction vids, meme edits of shareable Cards  
**Who posts:** Creators/users  
**Frequency:** Target 3–5 per week

### 7. YouTube/Podcasts
**Pre-launch:** Outreach to 10 mid-size creators for guest demo (“not fantasy/not prediction” story)  
**Launch week:** Inclusion in WC preview shows, demo Cards shown  
**Tournament:** “Copa Card of the Day” contest, promo code for group Card

**Content:**  
- Guest spots, game mechanic explainers, demo recap  
**Who posts:** Copa as guest/creator  
**Frequency:** 1–2 per week

### 8. Football Newsletters
**Pre-launch:** Submit UGC Card, “how fans saw VAR,” for possible inclusion  
**Launch week:** Offer aggregate crowd data for feature  
**Tournament:** “Copa Card for last night’s match” ready-to-drop assets

**Content:**  
- Commentary + Card inserts  
**Who posts:** Copa (B2B pitch)  
**Frequency:** Weekly, opportunistically
