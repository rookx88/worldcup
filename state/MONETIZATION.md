# Monetization

*How Copa makes money. This is a first-class concern — not a Phase 2 afterthought.*

## Constraint
- Zero budget for paid acquisition — monetization must work organically
- Must be live before or at tournament start (June 18, 2026)
- Must not break the core experience for free players — growth depends on free users sharing

## Status
**Unresolved — Copa must research and decide the primary monetization model.**

Copa should research how comparable products (daily fantasy, prediction games, sports social apps) actually make money, what conversion rates look like, and what players in the target audience have demonstrated willingness to pay for. Then pick a model based on evidence, not assumption.

## Open Questions
- What do casual sports prediction game players actually pay for (if anything)?
- What monetization models have worked for zero-budget viral sports apps?
- What has failed — and why?
- What is the right moment in the user journey to introduce a paywall or upsell?
- What price point has the least resistance in this audience?
