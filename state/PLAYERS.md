# Target Players

## Status
**Populated** — Generation 2 research. Three archetypes identified.

---

## Archetype 1: The Passionate Casual

**Name:** Sofia, 28
**Location:** Chicago (Mexican-American)
**Relationship to football:** Watches every Mexico match, follows major tournaments, doesn't follow club football
**Current behavior:** Live-tweets during matches. In a WhatsApp group with her cousins that goes insane during World Cup. Has never played DFS. Downloaded FIFA Fantasy once, found it confusing, deleted it.
**What she wants:** Something to do with her phone during the match that isn't just scrolling Twitter. Something to share with her cousins that makes the group chat more fun.
**What she'll pay for:** A Copa Card that looks beautiful in Mexico's green and white. Something she can post on Instagram and show her national pride.
**Why Copa Calls works for her:** No player knowledge required. She knows Mexico's energy. She can feel when they're about to score. Her instincts are the game.

---

## Archetype 2: The Competitive Fan Group Organizer

**Name:** Javier, 34
**Location:** Madrid
**Relationship to football:** Lifelong fan. Runs the office World Cup pool on a spreadsheet every four years. Knows it's a pain but does it anyway because everyone loves it.
**Current behavior:** Manually tracks predictions in Google Sheets. Sends WhatsApp updates to 15 people. Spends 2 hours setting up the pool, gets complaints about it not being fair.
**What he wants:** A real app that does what his spreadsheet does but better. Group competition. Automatic scoring. A reason for his group to stay engaged beyond "who wins the tournament."
**What he'll pay for:** Copa Pro because it lets him create a Crew and be the organizer. The Pro tier signals status within his group.
**Why Copa Calls works for him:** It solves his actual problem. He doesn't need to build it — Copa builds it for him. He just shares a link.

---

## Archetype 3: The Knowledge Flexer

**Name:** Marcus, 22
**Location:** London
**Relationship to football:** Deep. FPL addict. Watches 4-5 matches per week. Has opinions about every manager's tactical decisions.
**Current behavior:** Plays FPL, plays DraftKings during Premier League, active on r/soccer and r/FantasyPL. Frustrated that World Cup DFS is poor because soccer statistics are bad for salary-cap games.
**What he wants:** A game that rewards football *knowledge* — not just statistical optimization. He wants to be proven right about his instincts and flex that record publicly.
**What he'll pay for:** Copa Pro for the extended analytics ("you called set-piece goals at 78% — top 1% of Copa"). Stats that let him prove he knows the game better than his friends.
**Why Copa Calls works for him:** His knowledge actually helps — he knows which teams are dangerous at corners, which managers press high in the second half, which penalty takers choke. Copa Calls rewards football intelligence, not spreadsheet optimization.

---

## Notes on Audience Size

- **Sofia** is the largest segment by orders of magnitude. Billions of World Cup viewers fit this profile.
- **Javier** is the viral vector. He brings 15 Sofias. Acquisition cost effectively zero per Sofia.
- **Marcus** is the retention anchor and content creator. His Copa Card stats get shared in football communities and drive organic discovery.

The game must be designed so Sofia can play without reading instructions, Javier can set up a Crew in under 2 minutes, and Marcus finds enough depth to keep coming back for every match.
