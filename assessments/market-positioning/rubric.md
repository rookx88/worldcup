# Rubric: market-positioning

**What it measures:** How clearly Copa is positioned relative to competitors — who it's for, why it wins, why someone would choose it.

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.0–0.3 | No positioning defined |
| 0.3–0.5 | Vague positioning ("better fantasy for soccer fans") with no specific differentiation |
| 0.5–0.7 | Named target audience and one differentiator, but not fully developed |
| 0.7–0.9 | Clear "for X, not Y" positioning with named competitors and specific reasons to choose Copa |
| 0.9–1.0 | Fully articulated positioning: specific player type, specific gap vs competitors, memorable one-liner |

## Key Questions
- Is there a clear "who it's for and who it's NOT for" statement?
- Is there a named competitor Copa beats at something specific?
- Could a tweet or post convey the positioning in under 280 characters?
