# Rubric: marketing-reach

**What it measures:** How well Copa has mapped and begun activating zero-budget organic channels.

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.0–0.3 | No channels identified or mapped |
| 0.3–0.5 | General list of platforms with no specific strategy per channel |
| 0.5–0.7 | Specific communities and creators identified; content types mapped per channel |
| 0.7–0.9 | Prioritized channel list with content plan and timing; specific influencers in PIPELINE.md |
| 0.9–1.0 | Full pre-launch → launch → tournament content calendar; outreach drafted for top targets; clear activation sequence |

## Key Questions
- Are specific subreddits, Discord servers, or YouTube channels named (not just "Reddit" or "YouTube")?
- Is there a content plan that maps what to post and when?
- Are there specific people identified (not just "soccer influencers")?
