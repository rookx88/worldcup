# Rubric: viral-mechanics

**What it measures:** How effectively the game design creates organic sharing and friend invitation.

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.0–0.3 | No sharing mechanic designed |
| 0.3–0.5 | Generic "share your score" button — no compelling reason to share |
| 0.5–0.7 | Shareable result card designed, but sharing requires friction or login |
| 0.7–0.9 | Compelling share moment (upset pick, winning streak, friend duel) + low-friction join path |
| 0.9–1.0 | Every share creates a compelling invite loop; friend joining is frictionless; sharing feels like bragging, not marketing |

## Key Questions
- What is the specific moment a player feels compelled to share?
- What does someone see when they click a shared link?
- How many steps from "see a shared card" to "playing the game"?
